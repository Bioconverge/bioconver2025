BioConverge 2025

International BioE3 Conference – Bridging Biology, Engineering and Sustainability for Global Impact
& The 7th Annual Meeting of Society of Chemical and Synthetic Biology

Dates: October 6–7, 2025 | Workshop: October 8, 2025 (Gibson Assembly)
Venue: Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies

About the Conference

Bioconverge-25 brings together global experts to explore innovations at the intersection of biology, engineering, and sustainability. Hosted by MRIIRS in association with the Society of Chemical and Synthetic Biology, this conference aims to drive impactful interdisciplinary collaboration and provide sustainable solutions to global challenges.

Themes

  • Genomics and Synthetic Biology: Engineering Life for a Sustainable Future
  • Next-Gen Molecular Diagnostics for Health, Food Security and Biological Systems
  • Translational Bioengineering: Innovations in Disease, Aging, and Cancer
  • Host-Pathogen Evolution: Viral Blueprints and Immune Interventions
  • Synthetic and Chemical Biology 2.0: Designing the Future of Biomanufacturing
  • Bridging Biology, AI, Engineering, and Sustainability for Global Impact
